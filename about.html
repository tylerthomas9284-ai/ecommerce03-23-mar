About Clothiva

A womenswear brand shaped for fast-moving online retail.

Clothiva was designed as a modern, USA-targeted fashion storefront that balances trend speed, product clarity, payment flexibility, and service trust.

Our approach

Clothiva focuses on elevated essentials, event-ready fashion, curated drops, and mobile-friendly shopping journeys. The brand voice is confident, polished, and built for customers who want style without friction.

Mission

Deliver fashion-forward pieces with a storefront experience that feels trustworthy, efficient, and premium for US shoppers.

Customer promise

Clear product presentation, transparent policies, practical support, and flexible checkout options including COD where offered.
